PT. Hijnur Transport is an International/Domestic Freight Forwarding company established in Makassar since 2017 to your esteemed company. Our company has progressively expanded its scope of business, FCL, and LCL export and import, FCL sea domestic shipment all Indonesian ports, custom clearance, in-land transportation, warehousing, project cargo specialist, tug and barges and shipping agency. As a mayor player domestically, we are supported by our strong branch offices network and strong agents offices, which extends throughout Java, Sumatra, Kalimantan, and Sulawesi Island. Internationally, we are supported by our exclusive chain of our agencies in the world, each of whom is also a significant in their respective region. This advantage enables us providing competitive rates, while maintaining our quality of services.

SERVICES WE OFFER

Ocean Services (FCL/LCL)

We provide almost daily services from Jakarta port and others main ports in Indonesia to worldwide destination. Using mayor International shipping lines and offer diverse ocean freight services to meet customers satisfaction.

Domestic Services (FCL and Door to Door)

We provide almost daily of domestics services from Jakarta, Surabaya to others port in Sumatra, Kalimantan, Sulawesi, and Irian Jaya for FCL and Door to Door services. We are using mayor domestic shipping lines and also offer diverse ocean freight services to meet customers satisfaction.

Tug and Barges
